This article deliberately carries no inline image in its body, only prose, so the extraction keeps plenty of readable text and clears the character threshold comfortably while the lead picture lives outside the scored content — exactly the case where readability drops the hero image and the reader would otherwise show nothing but text with no picture at all.

A second paragraph continues with more ordinary sentences and clauses, adding enough length that the body is unambiguously the main content of the document and the extractor returns it as the article while the og:image remains the only picture the story has to offer the reader.
